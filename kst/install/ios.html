How to Install

Follow the steps below carefully. If you require assistance, please contact eduthing.

iOS (iPhone/iPad)

  1. Click the blue "Download" button above using SAFARI and click "Allow".
  2. Click "Close" and open the "Settings" app.
  3. Select "Profile Downloaded"
  4. Select "School WiFi Profile".
  5. Select "Install".
  6. Type in your passcode.
  7. Click "Install" > "Install" then "Done".
  8. Go to "Settings" > "General" > "About" > Scroll to the bottom and click "Certificate Trust Settings" > Enable "Root Certificate Authority".
  9. You can now join your school's guest WiFi.

Check that everything worked

After installation, browse to a familiar HTTPS site. If you see a padlock and no warnings, SSL decryption is ready. Clear cache or restart your device if prompts persist.
